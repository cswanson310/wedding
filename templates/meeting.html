    In 1992, Charlie and Lindsay were brought into this world as bumbling, big-boned babies.

    Just kidding, we won't go that far back. We just wanted to show off our baby pictures.
     Charlie and Lindsay met at a mutual friend's party while studying at Carnegie Mellon, and discovered they really liked each other! Lindsay told Charlie that she was from Maine, so Charlie proceeded to draw everything he knew about Maine on Lindsay's arm. This included, and was very possibly limited to, a pine tree, snowflakes, and a moose.
    Lindsay and Charlie were both friends with a group of people including Anshul, Sabrina, and Zack, and they found (or made) more opportunities for that group to get together that spring. This included a hiking excursion during which Charlie locked everyone out of the car and they had to wait for AAA, and a less strenuous trip to an apple orchard around Pittsburgh.
    Some time passed without another encounter, but eventually Lindsay and Charlie found themselves stranded with 10 of their friends in Times Square on New Years eve.     They received another hint from the universe when they discovered they had packed the same pair of L.L. Bean boots. Everyone had a great night, but Lindsay took off the next morning on a flight to Israel, so that didn't lead anywhere.
    Later that spring, Charlie invited a group of friends to spend the weekend at his Grandparent's house in Barnes, PA, about 3 hours north of Pittsburgh. They spent a fantastic day in the snow.     Just really perfected the sledding techniques.     Eventually, with a little prodding from Alex, Lindsay and Charlie hit it off and shared their first kiss in the cabin while Zack accompanied the snowfall with some melodies on the Piano.
