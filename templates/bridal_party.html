    ───── BRIDESMAIDS ─────

    Megan MacGillivray

    The Maid of Honor! Linday's younger sister Megan lives in Pittsburgh and is pursuing her masters in Mental Rehabilitation Health at Pitt University after earning her undergraduate degree there in Psychology. Megan is a fantastic Frisbee player, and helped get Pitt's team to Nationals 3 years in a row!

    Anna Swanson

    Charlie's older sister Anna and her adorable dog Chicory live in Glouchester, MA where she helps manage the Backyard Grower's program. She and her team help schools start their own gardens, help families start a backyard garden, and work to incorporate local (sometimes home-grown!) food into the school lunches.

    Katie Russel

    Katie and Lindsay have been friends since their middle school years at Molly Ockett in Fryeburg, Maine. Katie graduated from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in 2014 and now lives with her Fiancé Paul in Seattle. Lindsay and Katie have gone from homecoming dress shopping to wedding dress shopping together! Katie now works as a consultant at Deloitte, and helps coach a local youth ski team.

    Katy Marino

    Katy was a student at Carnegie Mellon with Charlie and Lindsay where they became good friends. She now lives in New York City where she works at Fradkin McAlpin Architects. Katy helps Lindsay and Charlie scout their way around the city looking for exciting places to eat and drink.

    Sabrina Larkin

    Sabrina and Lindsay were classmates in their freshman Chemical Engineering classes. They got to know each other well while working together organizing Alternative Spring Break (a community service trip) at Carnegie Mellon and were friends ever since. Sabrina got her degree in Chemical Engineering, and now lives in Boston where she works as a chemical engineer at YYY?

    Aurora Parlagrec

    Aurora was Lindsay's big sister during her years at Delta Delta Delta at Carnegie Mellon. She got to hear all about Charlie when he was still just Lindsay's crush, and offered superb romantic advice. She now lives in Brooklyn and works designing book covers at Harper Collins. You might recognize some of her work on young adult fiction covers such as the New York Times' Best-Seller Dumpling.

    Olivia Bello

    Olivia met Lindsay when she decided to join Teach For America after studying Biology at Agnes Scott College in Atlanta. She and Lindsay were classmates while pursuing their masters of education, and she and her boyfriend Kyle now live in Inwood with Lindsay and Charlie. She is the head of the science department and teaches eighth grade science at Kappa III School in the Bronx, and is helping the Billion Oyster Project restore oyster colonies around the New York harbor.

    ───── GROOMSMEN ─────

    Noel Swanson

    The best man! Charlie's younger brother Noel moved to Santa Monica, CA in 2016 to work in solar development at Cyprus Creek Renewables after graduating from Johns Hopkins with a degree in environmental science. He's known to post photos of trees to instagram.

    Ian MacGillivray

    Lindsay's younger brother Ian is a sophomore at Yarmouth High School in Yarmouth, ME. He loves performing in the school's fantastic plays and musicals, reads more books than you could imagine, and loves building things, like a hydroponic system where fish and plants feed each other.

    Jake Deitrich

    Jake was born as Charlie's best friend, and they spent many of their summers causing trouble at their neighboring lake houses in the Poconoes. Jake graduated from Hobart College with a degree in Political Science, and now works as an Admissions Counselor for Syracuse University.

    Zack Urbach

    Zack and Charlie were randomly paired as roommates during their freshman year at Carnegie Mellon, and hit it off so well that they lived together all four years! Zack graduated with a degree in Chemistry, and is now pursuing his PhD at Northwestern University after spending an intermediate 9 months doing Materials Science research in Mainz, Germany.

    Anshul Dhankher

    Anshul became friends with Lindsay during their freshman writing seminar at Carnegie Mellon, and became friends with Charlie soon after. He is a dedicated New England sports fan, and is famous for the great Carnival comeback of 2014. He worked two years as a biomedical engineer at Abbvie after graduating, and is now back in academia pursuing his PhD at Georgia Tech.

    Alex Smith

    Alex lived across from Charlie and Zack their freshman year of college, and always beat Charlie at tennis but lost in squash. Alex graduated with a degree in Business and a minor in computer science. He now works as a software engineer at Applied Predictive Technologies in Washington, D.C.

    Kyle Landau

    Kyle moved in with Olivia, Charlie and Lindsay after getting his degree in Architecture from Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo. He works as an Architect for SOAP in Brooklyn, and spends some of his time brewing the finest coffee in Inwood at Darling Coffee. He has strong opinions about chairs and is known to spoil Mr. Waffles with leftover turkey.
