    LODGING

        We have reserved many rental houses, all located near each other on Locust Lane. We encourage all guests to stay within the rental houses, where everything except the ceremony will be within walking distance. If you are more comfortable arranging for your own lodging, you are welcome to do so, but we would love if everyone would join us on-site.

    The Tent

        The tent will be on the rental property, and remain up all weekend. If you are staying with us at the rentals, it will be within walking distance.

    WELCOME DINNER

        Friday night is for dinner and dancing! Dinner, toasts & roasts will be buffet-style served starting at 6:00pm, and dancing will start at 8:00! Dinner will include lobster rolls, salads, and plenty of sides. It's a square dance/barn dance, so be prepared to learn a few moves.

    CEREMONY

        The ceremony will be held at Wildcat Mountain, all the way at the top! There will be gondolas to take you there. Should it rain, the ceremony will be in the lodge at the base of the mountain.

    (We're counting on less snow in September)
