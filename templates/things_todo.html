    HIKING

        Located in the middle of the White Moutains, North Conway is known for lots of incredible hiking trails. Some of our favorites include: Black Cap, Kersage, Cathedral Ledge, and Diana's Baths. There are many different levels of hikes from a short walk for the novice hiker to challenging hikes. For those looking for a full day hike, Mt. Washington is a short drive from North Conway. We have saved some of our favorite hikes on the map to the left.

    SHOPPING

        North Conway is home to the Settler's Green Outlets. Thanks to tax free shopping in New Hampshire, these outlets are a destination for many vistors in the Valley. 15 mintues from where Lindsay grew up, she spent a good amount of time with friends here in high school! You can use this link to find out more about stores, hours, etc. Besides the outlets, there are many other stores in North Conway including cute NH gift shops like Zeb's in the middle of town.
